# ISO 14971:2019 — Risk Management Process

## Process clauses
- **§4 General** — Plan, top management responsibility, qualified personnel
- **§5 Risk analysis** — Intended use, hazards, hazardous situations, harms
- **§6 Risk evaluation** — Are risks acceptable per the policy?
- **§7 Risk control** — Implement measures + verify implementation + verify effectiveness
- **§8 Evaluation of overall residual risk** — After all controls applied
- **§9 Risk management review** — Before commercial release
- **§10 Production and post-production activities** — Surveillance + feedback

## Mapping to QMS artifacts
- `docs/iec62304/03_Risk_Management_File.md` — primary RMF
- HAZ-NN identifiers describe hazardous situations
- RC-NN identifiers describe risk control measures
- Each RC must verify: (a) implementation present, (b) effectiveness measured
